# <font color="#2E86C1">🛍️ Blinkit Sales Performance Analysis</font>

## <font color="#2874A6">Project Overview</font>
This project features an interactive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze the sales performance and operational efficiency of **Blinkit**. The goal is to provide stakeholders with actionable insights into revenue growth, product fat content distribution, and outlet performance across different regions and sizes.

---

## <font color="#2874A6">🚀 Key Features & Insights</font>
* **Sales Tracking:** Real-time visibility into **Total Sales ($1.20M)** and **Average Sales ($141)**.
* **Product Segmentation:** Analysis of sales by **Item Type** and **Fat Content** (Low Fat vs. Regular).
* **Geographical Trends:** Performance breakdown across **Tier 1, 2, and 3 cities**.
* **Outlet Metrics:** Tracking sales growth by **Outlet Establishment Year** and **Outlet Size**.
* **Interactive Filters:** Dynamic slicers for real-time data exploration by location type, size, and year.

---

## <font color="#2874A6">🛠️ Technical Toolbox</font>
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Transformation:** Power Query (ETL process)
* **Theme:** Professional **Yellow and Dark-Grey** UI/UX design for an executive look.
